Featured

Includes a confusion matrix, per-class report, and an interface to classify new images from a file or URL.

AI agent for warehouse organization

In development

An AI agent that organizes a physical warehouse, both physically and digitally: tracking products, locations, quantities, measurements, and specifications, and supporting manufactured parts (products made up of other products, i.e. bills of materials or BOM). It answers natural-language questions about stock, location, and composition. Decoupled layered architecture: a deterministic core as the single source of truth, an LLM agent layer that interprets natural language without inventing data, interchangeable channels (CLI, web, and future Telegram and voice), and a future physical signaling layer with LEDs or lasers.
